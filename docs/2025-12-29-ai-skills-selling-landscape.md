## Executive Summary

The AI skills marketplace is a nascent but rapidly growing sector. Key findings:

- **Claude Code skills** are extremely early-stage with very few commercial sellers (notably @boringmarketer at $199)
- **Prompt marketplaces** are mature (PromptBase: 240K+ prompts, sellers keep 80%)
- **n8n/automation templates** are the hottest category on Gumroad ($20-30 for template packs)
- **Skool communities** dominate course distribution (top community: $335K/month)
- **MCP servers** remain mostly free with no major paid marketplace yet
- **Voice AI templates** (VAPI/Retell) are emerging at $30 price points

## Key Insights

### 1. Claude Code Skills Market is Nascent

- @boringmarketer is one of the very few commercial sellers at $199
- Most skills are shared freely via GitHub
- No official paid marketplace from Anthropic yet (mentioned as potential future)
- SkillsMP aggregates 26K+ free skills from GitHub

### 2. n8n/Automation Templates Are Hot

- Gumroad is the primary distribution platform
- Price points: $20-30 for template packs, €29.99 for mega packs
- Key sellers: Nate Herk, Nick Saraev, HubFlowCA, Usama Akram
- Focus areas: Lead gen, content repurposing, AI agents, cold outreach

### 3. Skool Communities Dominate Courses

- Liam Ottley's AAA Accelerator leads at $1,500
- Top community generates $335K/month
- 40% lifetime affiliate commissions
- Stephen Pope's No-Code Architects at $97/mo

### 4. MCP Servers Remain Mostly Free

- 17,250+ servers on mcp.so, most free
- 21st.dev is the only widely-cited monetization success ($20/mo)
- Official registries emerging but no paid marketplace yet
- Smithery.ai, MCPMarket.com building infrastructure

### 5. Prompt Marketplaces Are Mature

- PromptBase: 240K+ prompts, sellers keep 80%
- God of Prompt: Complete bundles at $150
- Etsy getting flooded with AI-generated content
- Image prompts (Midjourney, DALL-E) have largest demand

### 6. Voice AI Templates Are Emerging

- VAPI, Retell, Bland driving new category
- Price points around $30 on Gumroad
- White-label platforms (Vapify, Voicerr) for agencies
- Real estate, appointment setting most common use cases

### 7. Enterprise Marketplaces Launching

- AWS, Microsoft, Salesforce, IBM all launching agent marketplaces in 2025
- Focus on enterprise compliance and integration
- MCP and A2A protocols becoming standards

### 8. Revenue Models

| Model | Examples | Typical Take Rate |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| Marketplace commission | PromptBase, GPT Store | 20% |
| Course sales | Skool, Udemy | Platform varies |
| Template sales | Gumroad | 10% |
